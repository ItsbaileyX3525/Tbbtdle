How to Play

The Goal

You have 10 guesses to figure out which Big Bang Theory character is the answer for today.

How It Works

  • Start typing a character name - autocomplete will help you out
  • Hit Enter or click the play button to submit your guess
  • Each guess gives you clues about the target character
  • Use the clues to narrow down your next guess

Understanding the Colors

Green: Perfect match
Yellow: Close, but not quite (like same job field)

What Each Category Means

  • Character: Obviously, their name
  • Gender: Male, Female, or Other
  • Field: What they do for work or study
  • Relationship: Are they single, dating, married, etc?
  • First Appearance: When they first showed up (season and episode)

Some Tips

  • If you're stuck, try guessing main characters first
  • The relationship status and job clues are usually pretty helpful
  • First appearance can help you figure out if it's an early or late series character
  • Don't forget about recurring characters - it's not always the main cast!
